Xye & Mel Wedding

Designs I crafted for the wedding celebration of my beloved sister and her husband in December 2023.

SKILLS

Creative & Art Direction, Graphic Design & Layout, Print Media Design

TOOLBOX

Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator

The main theme of this wedding is the Arendelle Royal Wedding, drawing inspiration from the bride's favorite Disney Princess, Queen Elsa from the movie "Frozen". Consequently, the visual aesthetic revolves around winter elements such as snowflakes, ice, and the colors blue, white, and silver.

Invitation Design

3D-rendered image featuring the invitation placed on snow.

In crafting the wedding invitation, the couple aimed for an elegant design that seamlessly integrated with the Arendelle Royal Wedding theme. I incorporated a dark blue backdrop reminiscent of a glacier and snowy white text with subtle glows, evoking the magic of Queen Elsa's kingdom.

The printed copies were distributed with a white cover featuring a laser-cut snowflake texture, adding a touch of elegance and enchantment to the invitation ensemble.

Photo by Thal Ruin Photography.

Wedding Souvenirs

3D-rendered image featuring the souvenir coaster alongside a clear mug placed on snow.

I also crafted souvenirs for the guests, with the couple's love for coffee in mind. They selected a mug coaster as the main souvenir.

The design incorporates a scripture from the Bible, aiming to convey not only the love of the newlyweds but also God's love to all recipients of this gift. Remaining true to the theme, the coaster was designed to resemble a clear ice crystal.

LED Wall Display

I also designed the display for the LED Wall positioned on stage at the wedding reception. It prominently featured the names of the newlyweds, which doubled as the event's logo, adding a personalized touch to the celebration.
