Hunger (film)

A thought-provoking drama/horror short film addressing mental health, depression, and suicide. Through metaphorical scenes and captivating visuals, it encourages reflection and raises awareness.

Created as Post-Production Techniques final course requirement.

SKILLS

Creative & Art Direction, Cinematography, Video Editing, Sound Design, & Screenwriting

TOOLBOX

Figma, Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, & Audition

Synopsis

Hunger (2022) is a film about a college student's desperation to escape her painful condition. She struggles to decide how to cope with the problems when a mysterious person provides an easy solution.

Written, directed, and edited by Rae Cayabyab, this film is inspired by the Hatchetfield series (Team Starkid musicals available on YouTube.)
