Ignite | Unlock: Healing

IGNITE Conference is a quarterly event organized by Cavite City Foursquare Youth (CCFY). In 2021, the events were held fully-online through Facebook Live due to the pandemic.

SKILLS

Creative & Art Direction,, Digital Illustration, Graphic Design & Layout, Video Editing, Motion Graphics

TOOLBOX

Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, & After effects

As the Head of the Design Committee, I created various visuals for the event, including the main poster, title card, countdown posters, 5-minute countdown timer, motion graphics for transitions, and post-event publication materials.

For this particular installment of Ignite, we embraced a visual theme inspired by the jungle. Drawing inspiration from the film "Jumanji," I crafted our designs to evoke adventure and mystery.

Event Promotions

The 10-day countdown posters progressively illuminated with each passing day, symbolizing the growing fire burning within our hearts as the event drew nearer.

We also introduced our speakers in a style reminiscent of "Jumanji," where it appeared as though players were being selected for the adventure.
