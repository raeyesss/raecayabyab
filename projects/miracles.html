Miracles Worship Night

Miracles is a virtual worship night event organized by Cavite City Foursquare Youth (CCFY). It was conducted via Facebook Live in compliance with the pandemic quarantine regulations at the time.

SKILLS

Creative & Art Direction,, Digital Illustration, Graphic Design & Layout, Video Editing, Motion Graphics

TOOLBOX

Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, & After effects

As the Head of the Design Committee, I spearheaded the creation of various visuals for the event, including the main poster, title card, countdown posters, 5-minute countdown timer, motion graphics for transitions, and post-event publication materials.

Main Poster

For this event, we embraced a visual theme of "acoustic summer," evident in the wood texture resembling a cottage with a poster placed on it, creating the impression of a beach resort event announcement. The poster also features musical instruments commonly associated with summer outings. Subtle shadows of palm tree leaves add depth and immersion, enhancing the experience of viewing the poster.
