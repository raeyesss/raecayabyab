Genesis 2022 | Ever-Evolving: Phases and Faces

A collaboration between students of the Project Management course, "Ever-Evolving: Phases and Faces" served as the 4th installment of the Genesis Multimedia Arts Portfolio Exhibit at De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde. It had its website launch on August 11, 2022.

SKILLS

Creative & Art Direction, Graphic Design & Layout, Web design, Video Editing, Motion Graphics, Social Media Advertising, Project Management, and Communication

TOOLBOX

Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, & After Effects, and Discord

Main Exhibit: Website

Similar to previous launches, this website featured diverse Benildean Artists from the MMPPEXD (Portfolio Exhibit Course) along with their works. What sets this installment apart is that each exhibitor created character designs representing their growth as multimedia artists. Each character was initiated from a basic shape (circle, triangle, square), which was assigned according to the exhibitor's section.

Facebook Page

The production team held a week-long social media promotion campaign which included official page branding, DP blast, announcement & engagement posts, countdown, and video interview/vlogs.

The project proved to be successful, despite encountering minor technical issues.

Please note that our website is currently offline due to a technical issue with our Wordpress subscription. However, all campaign promotions remain accessible through our official Facebook page.
