Cluster Camp: "Pagsibol"

A 3-day survival camp organized by the Foursquare Church - Dove & Crown Divisions.

SKILLS

Creative & Art Direction, Graphic Design & Layout, Typography, Photography, Videography, Project Management, and Communication

TOOLBOX

Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom & Illustrator, Nikon D3400 with Yongnuo 50mm 1.8 Lens

Official Merch Design

As a Media Specialists member, I lead the design efforts for our official merchandise, which included singlets, dog tags, and sticker sets. Utilizing social media platforms, we effectively promoted these products, offering pre-orders to our audience.

In order to simplify the ordering procedure, we created an online form and I crafted a detailed size chart for singlets, ensuring a smooth and user-friendly experience for customers.

Facebook DP Blast

I was also tasked with creating a frame for the Facebook DP blast, to be utilized by staff, pastors, and potential camp attendees.

Documentation

My largest responsibility involved documenting the entire event. Alongside my team, we captured photos and videos of the sessions, games, and various activities throughout the camp. Some of my favorite shots from Pagsibol can be viewed via Behance.

I also contributed to co-producing the video highlights for post-event promotion, which is available for viewing on Facebook.
